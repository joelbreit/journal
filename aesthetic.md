# Warm/Personal Aesthetic Guide

## Philosophy
Create an inviting, intimate space for reflection that feels human and analog—like sitting down with a good notebook rather than opening another productivity tool.

## Color Palette

### Backgrounds
- Primary: `#FAF8F5` (cream/linen)
- Gradient: `from-amber-50 to-orange-50`
- Card backgrounds: White with warm tints

### Accents
- Primary action: `#D4886C` (terracotta/rose-400)
- Secondary: `#A8B5A0` (sage)
- Tertiary: `#C9A7A3` (dusty rose)

### Text
- Primary: `#78350F` (amber-950)
- Secondary: `#92400E` (amber-800/700)
- Muted: Amber-600/500

### Tag Colors (muted, sun-faded)
- Personal: Rose-200/900
- Work: Amber-200/900
- Gratitude: Emerald-200/900
- Goals: Blue-200/900
- Reflection: Purple-200/900

## Typography

### Body Content (Entries)
- Font: **Crimson Text** or **Lora** (serif)
- Size: 18px (text-lg)
- Line height: 1.75 (leading-relaxed)
- Weight: Regular (400)

### Titles
- Font: Serif (same as body)
- Size: 36px (text-4xl) for entry titles
- Weight: Bold (700)

### UI Elements
- Font: **Inter** or **Source Sans** (sans-serif)
- Size: 14px (text-sm) for labels, buttons
- Weight: Medium (500) for emphasis

## Spacing & Layout

### Borders
- Radius: 8-12px (rounded-lg to rounded-xl)
- Color: amber-200/300 (subtle, warm)

### Shadows
- Soft, natural: `shadow-sm`, `shadow-md`
- No harsh drop shadows
- Optional: `shadow-amber-900/10` for warmth

### Padding
- Generous:min 16px (p-4) for cards
- Content: 32px (p-8) for main editor
- Line height: Always relaxed (1.75+)

## Interactive States

### Hover
- Slight opacity reduction (80-90%)
- Background darkening (e.g., bg-amber-50 → bg-amber-100)
- No harsh transitions

### Active/Selected
- Background: amber-100
- Add subtle shadow
- Optional: ring-2 ring-rose-400 for tags

### Transitions
- Duration: 300-400ms (smooth, gentle)
- Easing: Default or ease-in-out
- Never snappy or instant

## Component Patterns

### Buttons
- Primary: Rose-400 background, white text, rounded-lg
- Secondary: Border with amber-300, text amber-900
- Hover: Darken by one shade

### Tags
- Pill shape (rounded-full)
- Muted color backgrounds with darker text
- Small text (text-sm)
- Padding: px-3 py-1

### Cards/Entries
- White or cream background
- Rounded corners (8-12px)
- Border: 1px amber-200
- Hover: Slight background shift

## What to Avoid
- Pure white (#FFFFFF) backgrounds
- High saturation colors
- Sharp corners or harsh shadows
- Monospace fonts (except for code blocks)
- Bright neon or primary colors
- Excessive decoration or skeuomorphism
- Snappy animations (<200ms)

## Design Principles
1. **Warmth over efficiency** - Prioritize comfort and invitation
2. **Breathing room** - Never cramped, always spacious
3. **Soft edges** - Rounded, gentle, organic
4. **Natural light** - Colors feel sun-touched, not digital
5. **Timelessness** - Classic, not trendy
